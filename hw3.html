Golden State Warriors

Golden State Warriors franchise was founded in 1946. The team was relocated from Philadelphia to San Francisco in 1962. After spending over a decade in Philadelphia, the Warriors made the move to the bay area and became the San Francisco Warriors.
They played home games at the iconic Cow Palace before eventually moving to Oracle Arena in Oakland and then to Chase Center in San Francisco. The Golden State Warriors have won 7 NBA championships. They are the third team to have most championships and finals appearances. Their titles came in the years 1947, 1956, 1975, 2015, 2017, 2018, and 2022.

San Francisco 49ers

San Francisco 49ers have rich history of success, with five Superbowl championships. With victories in Superbowl XVI, XIX, XXIII, XXIV, and XXIX, the 49ers have earned themselves as one of the most successful teams in NFL history. The 49ers was established in 1946. Candlestick Park in San Francisco was the first stadium 49ers played at and won five superbowls during that time. In 2013, the San Francisco county have decided to demolished the stadium to create more housing in the bayview neighborhood. The San Francisco 49ers team was relocated to a new stadium in Santa Clara called Levi's Stadium, as their new home.
